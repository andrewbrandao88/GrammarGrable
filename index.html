Background

It is important from a young age to develop reading and comprehension skills to function in society. In everyday life people communciate and should communicate with proper linguistic skills. Our project aims to help develop reading skills in students in an engaging and applicable way.

Description

Students will play as Dajan, a student at the boarding school Lowell School of Technical Greatness, trying to sneak out of the dorms. To achieve this, players will walk around finding answers, keys, to solve a poetic riddle to help their reading and comprehension skills, needing to discern the correct answer for the correct key.
